Iron Man (Tony Stark):

A genius billionaire and inventor, Tony Stark becomes Iron Man by building a high-tech suit of armor, using his intelligence and resources to fight evil.

Captain America (Steve Rogers):

super-soldier enhanced by a special serum, Steve Rogers is a World War II hero who fights for justice with his indestructible shield.

Thor:

The Norse god of thunder, Thor wields the mystical hammer Mjolnir and protects both Earth and Asgard with his immense strength and control over lightning.

Nick Fury:

A high-ranking S.H.I.E.L.D. director, Nick Fury is a master strategist who brings together heroes like the Avengers to defend the world from threats.
