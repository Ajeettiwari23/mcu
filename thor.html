Thor

Thor:


The god of thunder and the prince of Asgard, Thor is a powerful warrior who wields the enchanted hammer Mjolnir. Initially brash and impulsive, Thor learns humility and wisdom throughout his adventures. He protects both Earth and the Nine Realms from cosmic threats, including his brother Loki. With god-like strength and control over lightning, Thor is a central figure in the Avengers and a protector of the universe.
