Spider Man

Spider-Man (Peter Parker):


Peter Parker, a high school student, gains superhuman abilities after being bitten by a radioactive spider. With enhanced strength, agility, and the ability to stick to walls, he creates his web-shooters to swing through New York City and fight crime as Spider-Man. Guided by the motto "With great power comes great responsibility," Peter balances his life as a superhero with his personal struggles. His intelligence, quick wit, and sense of duty make him one of the most beloved and relatable heroes in the Marvel Universe.
