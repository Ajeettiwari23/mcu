Iron Man

Iron Man (Tony Stark):


Tony Stark is a brilliant inventor and billionaire industrialist who created the Iron Man suit after being captured by terrorists. With his advanced technology and intellect, he fights against global threats and leads Stark Industries. His journey from a self-centered playboy to a selfless hero is central to his character. He is a founding member of the Avengers and often uses his resources to fund and equip the team. Despite his flaws, Tony is deeply committed to protecting humanity.
