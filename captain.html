Captain America

Captain America (Steve Rogers):


A frail young man from Brooklyn, Steve Rogers was transformed into the perfect soldier through a super-soldier serum during World War II. Known for his unwavering morals and sense of justice, he fights evil as Captain America with his indestructible shield. After being frozen in ice, Steve awakes in the modern era and becomes the leader of the Avengers. He represents the values of heroism, sacrifice, and hope.
